Thinking

ThinkLikeGeek helps your brain to think better. By doing courses on ThinkLikeGeek regularly, you never stop learning, so your brain doesn't stabilize itself much, which means, you have a strong foundation for learning. By this, we mean that, the more you learn using ThinkLikeGeek, the stronger your brain gets, and the faster it takes to learn other new things. Not only does using ThinkLikeGeek help your brain to grasp new, important concepts, but it also promotes the power to think.

Smiling

Have you ever thought that learning should not be what the teachers want to teach, but what the learners want to learn? It's very logical that one who doesn't go to school, but does programming all day, and gets a programming job, will do much better than who goes to school, studies what is given, and gets a programming job. Which is why, ThinkLikeGeek doesn't want you to learn with an 'ugh, this is boring' face, but a smile.
